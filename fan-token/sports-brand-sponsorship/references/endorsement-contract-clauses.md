# Sports Endorsement Contract Clause Library

## Standard clauses

Morality/Conduct clause: brand terminates if athlete damages reputation.
Athlete protection: define "materially damages" precisely. Mutual clause recommended.

Image rights licence: defines images, media, territories, time period.
Scope: "in perpetuity" is unfavourable — push for deal term + 12 months max.
Digital: ensure social/digital use rights explicitly defined.

Approval rights: athlete right to approve creative before publication.
Minimum: 5 business day review window. Strong position: one rejection per campaign.

Exclusivity: typically 12–24 months. Category exclusive prevents all competitor deals.
Important: define "category" narrowly (e.g., "athletic footwear" not "fashion").

Social media obligations: frequency, platform, approval process.
Must include: ASA (UK) / FTC (US) disclosure compliance (#ad labelling).

## Performance-linked clauses

Appearance bonus: triggered by 30+ starts in season. Include injury exception.
Achievement bonus: league title, Champions League, individual awards. Cap at 30–50% uplift.
Social milestone: reaching X followers. Specify organic growth verification method.
Token holder activation bonus (new 2024–26): triggered when fan token campaign exceeds X% participation. Blockchain-verified, objective, tamper-proof.

## Termination triggers

For cause (brand): criminal conviction, conduct breach, drugs test, injury preventing deliverables.
Fair: 30-day cure period except criminal conviction.

Convenience: brand typically has 90-day notice exit + 50% remaining fee payment.
Athlete: 90-day notice (brand usually holds this power exclusively).

Performance triggers: drops below X appearances / national team squad.
Athlete protection: injury exception clause is non-negotiable.
