# Core Metric Definitions — performance-on-pitch

## Offensive
npxG (non-penalty expected goals): shot quality probability model. Higher = better chances.
xG Overperformance (G-xG): goals minus xG. Positive = clinical. >+3 per season = skill signal.
Shot on Target %: SoT / total shots. Elite forwards >45%.
Progressive Carries per 90: carries advancing ball ≥10m toward goal. Elite: >5 per 90.
Dribble Success %: completed / attempted dribbles. Efficient >60%; below 40% = possession loss.
SCA (Shot-Creating Actions): two actions before a shot. Per 90. Elite creators: >4 per 90.
Key Passes per 90: passes directly creating a shot attempt. Distinct from xA.
xA per 90: expected goals from assisted shots. Elite: >0.25 per 90.

## Defensive
Pressures per 90: pressure attempts on ball carrier. Compare within position and system only.
Pressure Success Rate: % pressures leading to turnover. High volume + high success = elite.
Tackles Won per 90: successful dispossessions. Elite DMs: >2.5 per 90.
Interceptions per 90: balls intercepted. Elite CBs/DMs: >1.5 per 90.
Aerials Won %: aerial duels won. Strong CBs: >60%. Strong strikers: >55%.
Errors Leading to Shots: mistakes causing opponent shots. Flag: >0.3 per 90.

## Passing & Build-Up
Pass Completion %: context-dependent. Elite CMs: 88–94%. GKs playing out: ~80%.
Progressive Passes per 90: passes advancing ball ≥10m toward goal. Elite: >6 per 90.
Through balls per 90: completed passes behind defensive line. Rare: >0.3 = elite.
Long Ball Completion %: passes >32m. Good: >60%. Elite distribution: >70%.

## Goalkeeper Specific
PSxG - GA: post-shot xG minus goals against. Best measure of shot-stopping. +3 per season = significant.
Save %: saves / shots on target. Context limited — PSxG is better.
Crosses Claimed %: >8% claimed = comfortable; <5% = exploitable weakness.
Distribution Accuracy: short range >80%; long range >55% is good.
