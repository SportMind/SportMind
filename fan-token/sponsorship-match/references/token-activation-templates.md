# Token-Native Activation Templates by Brand Category

## Sportswear & Footwear
Template: Product co-creation via fan vote
- Fan token holders vote on athlete's exclusive colourway or design element
- Limited production run; top voters receive priority purchase or free pair
- Brand gets: authentic co-creation data, global PR hook, measurable holder engagement
- Track: poll participation rate vs. baseline, TVI during campaign, social amplification

## Crypto / Web3 Platforms
Template: Performance-linked rewards
- Athlete milestone (goal, assist, clean sheet) triggers automatic token holder reward on platform
- E.g., every goal = platform credits distributed pro-rata to holders who've signed up
- Brand gets: new user acquisition with sports audience, authentic Web3 story
- Track: new user signups correlated to athlete match days, holder activation rate

## Energy Drinks / Food & Beverage
Template: Matchday gating
- Exclusive discount code or offer delivered to fan token holders on matchday
- Time-limited (24h post-match win only) creates urgency
- Brand gets: measurable conversion from engaged sports fans, geo-targeted (holder geography)
- Track: redemption rate vs. non-token control group, TVI correlation to campaign days

## Mobile & Electronics
Template: Behind-the-scenes access exchange
- Fan token holders unlock exclusive athlete content filmed on/with the product
- Training ground footage, pre-match routine — content that requires the product naturally
- Brand gets: premium placement in authentic athlete content, global reach via holder base
- Track: content engagement rate, token holder growth during campaign, brand mention sentiment

## Financial Services / Fintech
Template: Fan token staking with brand yield
- Brand co-sponsors a staking programme where fan token holders earn yield in brand currency
- E.g., stake 10 BAR for 30 days → earn €5 in platform credits
- Brand gets: direct financial relationship with sports fans, sticky engagement
- Track: staking volumes, platform sign-ups, CHZ/token velocity during programme

## Automotive
Template: Matchday experience lottery
- Fan token holders enter automatic lottery for match-day VIP experience delivered in branded car
- "Arrive like [Athlete]" — chauffeur to stadium in [Brand] vehicle
- Brand gets: premium experiential association with athlete and club
- Track: token holder activity spike around lottery draws, social sharing from winners

## Travel & Hospitality
Template: Away trip packages for holders
- Token holders get preferential access or discount on official away travel packages
- Curated trips to away Champions League games with athlete meet-and-greet element
- Brand gets: high-value customer acquisition (sports fans travelling = premium demographic)
- Track: package redemption rate, holder geography vs. away fixture locations

---

## General principles for token-native activations

1. **Exclusivity first** — The activation must be impossible without holding the token.
   Generic discounts available elsewhere kill the value proposition.

2. **Tie to athlete milestones** — Performance-triggered rewards create authentic narrative.
   Brands want to be associated with success, not just attendance.

3. **Measure in token terms** — ROI measured in HAS change, poll participation lift,
   and TVI response — not just impressions. This is SportMind's differentiation.

4. **Layer social amplification** — Athlete posts about the activation during/after.
   Holder response to that post is doubly measurable (AELS × campaign).

5. **Geographic targeting** — Use holder geography to target the brand's priority markets.
   A Brazilian-skewed holder base is perfect for a brand expanding in LatAm.
