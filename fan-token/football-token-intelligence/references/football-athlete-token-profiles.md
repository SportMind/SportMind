## How to read this file

Each profile contains:
- **Club token(s):** which fan token(s) this athlete is associated with
- **ATM score:** Athlete Token Multiplier (0.00–1.50) — their systemic token importance
- **ATM peak competition:** where their ATM is highest
- **Token holder nationality overlap:** which national holder communities they activate
- **NCSI clubs:** which national team token they also affect (spillover)
- **Key signal patterns:** documented ATM behaviours from historical data

## Tier: Elite ATM (1.20–1.50)

### Profile type: Global brand forward at European super-club
**Archetype:** Brazilian / Argentine / French forward, 25–30 years old, at PSG / Barcelona / Real Madrid / Juventus
**ATM range:** 1.25–1.45
**ATM peak competition:** UCL knockout + World Cup (dual-token amplification)
**Key pattern:**
- 28–34% of club token holders share player's nationality — "bought the token for the player" dynamic
- Social posts in player's primary language activate holders who are otherwise passive
- Goals in UCL knockout rounds generate TVI spikes 3–5× larger than equivalent domestic goals
- Transfer rumours elevate ATM temporarily to 1.40+ for rumour-attributed period
**NCSI note:** When national team competes in WC/Euros, club token absorbs NCSI multiplier ×0.65–0.85

### Profile type: Iconic club captain, one-club career or long-tenure legend
**Archetype:** Midfielder or forward, 28–34 years old, 5+ years at same club, club identity figure
**ATM range:** 1.20–1.38
**ATM peak competition:** Domestic title decider, UCL final, retirement announcement
**Key pattern:**
- Token holders bought in because of this player — departure = structural ATM collapse
- Injury return from serious setback generates debut-premium equivalent (+6–15%)
- Consistent moderate ATM across all competitions (less competition-variance than global brand type)
- Retirement or departure is the highest single negative ATM event for this archetype
**NCSI note:** National team participation still generates club spillover but at lower NCSI than global brand type

## Tier: High ATM (1.00–1.19)

### Profile type: Clinical striker at established UCL club
**Archetype:** Forward, 22–28 years old, strong goals-per-90 metrics, UCL-regular club
**ATM range:** 1.00–1.18
**ATM peak competition:** UCL knockout rounds, El Clásico / major derby equivalents
**Key pattern:**
- On-pitch performance drives ATM more than social — a hat-trick in UCL moves token predictably
- ATM drops sharply during goal drought (3+ games without goal = ATM -0.15 temporarily)
- Recovery from goal drought with UCL hat-trick = ATM overshoot on upside (+0.20 above baseline)
- Moderate nationality overlap in token base — not the "bought for the player" dynamic

### Profile type: High-profile goalkeeper with distribution emphasis
**Archetype:** Goalkeeper, 24–32 years old, top-3 European club, excellent ball-playing ability
**ATM range:** 1.00–1.12
**ATM peak competition:** UCL knockout, penalty shootout situations (specific event premium)
**Key pattern:**
- Goalkeeper ATM is largely flat across competitions BUT spikes dramatically for:
  - Penalty shootout saves in UCL knockout: +25–40% TVI spike on save
  - Distribution statistics showing up in viral analytics content
  - Club keeping clean sheet in high-FTIS fixture
- Injury to high-ATM goalkeeper is a higher-impact event than equivalent outfield injury
  (one dedicated position, no rotation logic)

## Tier: Strong ATM (0.80–0.99)

### Profile type: Creative midfielder, assist machine
**Archetype:** Central or attacking midfielder, 23–30 years old, high key passes and xA
**ATM range:** 0.80–0.98
**ATM peak competition:** UCL group and knockout, high-profile domestic matches
**Key pattern:**
- ATM activates strongly when team is performing well (assists accumulate)
- Individual performance can temporarily offset negative team result on token
- "Assist in a loss" = muted token response (poor overall signal context)
- Social media content focusing on training clips and tactical content performs above average

### Profile type: Young domestic talent breaking through
**Archetype:** Midfielder or forward, 18–22 years old, first full senior season, academy product
**ATM range:** 0.75–0.95 (with high upside trajectory)
**ATM peak competition:** First UCL starts, debut national team call-up
**Key pattern:**
- ATM trajectory is steeply rising — current score understates likely near-future impact
- First national team cap creates debut premium for club token (NCSI ×0.30 — stronger than standard for young player)
- Social media following growing rapidly — AELS will increase as follower base matures
- Transfer speculation dramatically elevates ATM temporarily (youth talent rumours = narrative premium)

Primary club token connections:

- Key signal: German holder base is domestic-heavy — GER token more domestically driven than ARG/BRA

### USMNT (USA national token)
Primary club token connections:
- Lower direct club token NCSI than European nations — fewer USMNT players at fan-token clubs
- Key signal: Host nation premium — significant new North American holder acquisition expected during tournament
- 2026 context: Host nation, growing football culture, first major home tournament — USMNT token has highest narrative upside of any national token regardless of sporting result
