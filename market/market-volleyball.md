# Volleyball — SportMind Market Intelligence

## At a Glance
**Tier 3 — Viable but Longer Horizon.** Volleyball is the third most participated sport
globally with ~800M players, but professional spectatorship is concentrated in specific
national markets (Brazil, Poland, Italy, Russia/Soviet bloc tradition). No fan tokens.
Beach volleyball is a separate commercial property with stronger Olympic appeal. The sport's
Brazilian connection is its most commercially distinctive global characteristic.

---

## Fan Token™ Readiness Tier

**Tier 3 — Viable but Longer Horizon**

Volleyball's participation numbers are extraordinary (800M players) but spectatorship and
commercial infrastructure are significantly smaller. Brazil's Superliga is the highest-quality
domestic competition and Brazilian players dominate global marketing. The FIVB Nations League
(annual; replaced the World League) provides a sustained international competition calendar
that other Olympic sports lack.

---

## 1. Global Revenue and Broadcasts

**FIVB (global governing body):** ~$100M/yr
**Domestic leagues combined (Brazil, Italy, Poland, Russia):** ~$200M/yr
**Olympics volleyball:** Embedded in Olympic broadcast deals; beach volleyball generates
strong viewership

**Key broadcast:** GloboSports (Brazil); Sky Italia; Polsat (Poland); DAZN (international streaming)

---

## 2. Fanbase and Demographics

**Global fan estimate:** ~800M (participation-based; spectator interest varies widely)
**Core spectator markets:** Brazil (~50M fans), Poland (~10M), Italy (~8M), Serbia (~3M)
**Age profile:** Median age ~29; younger than most sports in library
**Gender split:** ~50% male / ~50% female
**Brazil factor:** The Brazilian national volleyball team (men's and women's) is one of
the most successful in history — Brazilian volleyball generates extraordinary domestic passion

---

## 3. Token Catalyst Events

**Brazil volleyball club token:** Brazilian Superliga clubs (Sada Cruzeiro, Taubaté)
in the market with highest volleyball passion is the most plausible entry.
**FIVB Nations League expansion:** Growing calendar creates more activation windows than
many Olympic sports.

---

## 4. Agent Context Summary

- Brazil is volleyball's most commercially significant market — any token programme should
  prioritise Brazilian club or national team tokens
- Beach volleyball (separate commercial property from indoor) has stronger Olympic appeal
  and a more affluent/lifestyle audience — potentially more token-suitable
- FIVB Nations League's year-round structure (unlike Olympics-only sports) provides sustained engagement
