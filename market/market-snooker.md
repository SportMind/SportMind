# Snooker — SportMind Market Intelligence

## At a Glance
**Tier 3 — Viable but Longer Horizon.** Snooker has undergone remarkable commercial
growth under World Snooker Tour (World Snooker / Matchroom Sport ownership), with the
Crucible World Championship an iconic BBC event and a growing Chinese market making it
the most geographically interesting niche sport in the library. No fan tokens. The
Chinese connection is snooker's most commercially distinctive characteristic.

---

## Fan Token Readiness Tier

**Tier 3 — Viable but Longer Horizon**

Snooker's commercial scale (~$80M WST revenue) is too small for Tier 2 standalone.
However, the sport has an extraordinary characteristic: China has ~250M snooker followers,
and Chinese players (Ding Junhui, Zhao Xintong before ban) have generated enormous
engagement in the world's largest middle-class consumer market. A China-facing snooker
token would tap into a completely different commercial universe than the UK/European core.

---

## 1. Global Revenue and Broadcast Deals

**World Snooker Tour revenue (est.):** ~$80M/yr
**Key deals:** BBC (World Championship — free-to-air UK, massive reach); Eurosport;
CCTV5 (China — state broadcaster, reaching ~250M potential viewers)
**China market value:** CCTV5 partnership makes snooker one of the most-broadcast
Western sports in China — the commercial potential is significantly larger than current revenue suggests

---

## 2. Fanbase Size, Geography, and Demographic Profile

**Global fan estimate:** ~500M (China's ~250M followers make this number exceptional for a "niche" sport)
**UK fans:** ~8M; China: ~250M; rest of world: ~50M
**Age profile:** UK median ~43; China median ~30 (significantly younger)
**Chinese demographic:** Snooker's Chinese fanbase is younger, urban, and more digitally
sophisticated than its UK counterpart — the most token-relevant segment in the sport by far

---

## 3. Digital and Social Engagement Index

**China:** Snooker is among the most-searched sports topics on Weibo and Baidu in China
**Ronnie O'Sullivan:** The sport's most globally marketable player has strong Chinese following
**Crucible atmosphere:** The World Championship's unique atmosphere (18-day event, tradition,
BBC coverage) creates one of sport's most distinctive broadcast products

---

## 4. Institutional Blockchain and Fan Token Interest

**World Snooker Tour (Matchroom Sport):** Commercially aggressive (Barry Hearn's organisation);
has run digital engagement experiments; more likely than most governing bodies to engage
with token products if commercial case is clear
**China factor:** A Chinese-platform snooker token (WeChat Pay integration, Alipay wallet)
would be a completely different product to a Western Chiliz token

---

## 5. Active Fan Token Ecosystem

**Current status:** None
**Most plausible:** Ronnie O'Sullivan legacy token; Judd Trump; Chinese player tokens if
regulatory environment permits. The Crucible (World Championship venue) as a brand token
is conceptually interesting — one of sport's most recognisable single venues.

---

## 6. Token Catalyst Events

**Chinese player World Championship contention:** A Chinese world champion would be the
single largest commercial event in snooker's history — triggering immediate Chinese token interest
**Matchroom digital strategy:** Barry Hearn's commercial track record (built PDC darts, PBC boxing)
includes digital product development as standard practice

---

## 7. Agent Context Summary

- China's 250M snooker followers is the defining commercial characteristic — this is a
  "niche" sport globally with a massive market in one country
- The 147 maximum break (see domain skill) is snooker's catalytic individual event —
  generates immediate viral coverage equivalent to a hole-in-one
- Crucible record (see athlete skill) is the most venue-specific predictive variable in
  the library — some players perform dramatically differently at the World Championship
