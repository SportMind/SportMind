# Table Tennis — SportMind Market Intelligence

## At a Glance
**Tier 3/4 — China-Concentrated.** Table tennis is dominated by China to a degree
unmatched by any other sport — the Chinese national team has won ~70% of all Olympic
gold medals in the sport. Outside China, commercial scale is very small. The ITTF World
Tour and WTT (World Table Tennis) have been attempting to commercialise the sport
globally but progress is slow.

---

## Fan Token Readiness Tier

**Tier 3/4 — China-Concentrated**

Table tennis is Tier 3/4 because its enormous Chinese audience (estimated 300M players)
operates within a commercial and regulatory ecosystem controlled by the Chinese government,
where Western fan token platforms (Chiliz) have no access. Outside China, commercial
scale is too small for Tier 3.

---

## 1. Global Revenue and Broadcasts

**WTT (World Table Tennis):** Commercial rights consolidated post-2021; revenue estimates ~$30M/yr
**Chinese domestic market:** CCTV coverage; enormous but not directly accessible by Chiliz-type platforms
**Olympic table tennis:** Strong viewership in China and East Asia; embedded in Olympic deals

---

## 2. Fanbase and Demographics

**Global fan estimate:** ~850M (China-concentrated)
**China:** ~500M fans/participants; table tennis is a genuine national sport
**East Asia broadly:** Japan (~30M), South Korea (~20M), Germany (~5M)
**Age profile:** Broadly distributed; participation sport with all ages; spectatorship younger in China

---

## 3. Token Catalyst Events

**WTT international expansion:** If WTT successfully creates a genuinely global commercial
structure with non-China-controlled commercial rights, the sport's global token potential improves.
**Non-Chinese dominance:** When a non-Chinese player wins an Olympic gold (extremely rare),
it is the sport's highest-impact commercial event globally.

---

## 4. Agent Context Summary

- Table tennis is Tier 3/4 due to China-platform incompatibility — the sport's enormous
  audience lives inside a commercial ecosystem that Chiliz cannot currently access
- Outside China, commercial scale is too small for token viability
- WTT's international commercialisation efforts are the variable to monitor for tier upgrade
