# Euroleague Basketball — Commercial Intelligence

**Fan token and commercial intelligence for European basketball clubs
operating in the EuroLeague, EuroCup, and national leagues.**

European basketball occupies a distinct commercial space from the NBA.
The fan base is passionate, regional, and increasingly digitally engaged.
Several EuroLeague clubs have or are developing fan token programmes,
and the sport's club loyalty culture makes it well-suited to token economics.

## EuroLeague competition intelligence

```
COMPETITION STRUCTURE:
  Regular season: 18 clubs × 34 matches (home/away)
  Top 8 advance to Playoffs (best-of-5 series)
  Final Four: 4-team event (usually mid-May, rotating city)
  Champion crowned at Final Four

  SIGNAL CALENDAR:
    October-March: Regular season (lower individual match signal)
    April Playoffs: Best-of-5 series; signal escalates each round
    Final Four (May): Maximum EuroLeague signal
    
    EuroLeague Final Four signal weight: × 1.75 (equivalent to domestic cup final)

PLAYOFF SERIES DYNAMICS:
  EuroLeague uses best-of-5 series (not single elimination)
  Series momentum matters: team winning Game 1 wins series 68% historically
  Game 5 (if reached): elimination game modifier × 1.08 (same as NBA)
  
  SERIES SIGNAL MODEL:
    Tied 2-2: both teams maximum motivation → × 1.12 on standard signal
    Leading 3-0: series sweep imminent → apply rest modifier × 0.92 for next round

FINAL FOUR HOST CITY BONUS:
  The Final Four rotates to different European cities
  Host city club (if relevant): × 1.10 hometown advantage
  Major market (Barcelona, Istanbul, Athens): × 1.08 crowd atmosphere

EUROLEAGUE vs NATIONAL LEAGUE:
  EuroLeague clubs must balance EuroLeague and domestic league
  Rotation signal: after EuroLeague match mid-week,
                    apply × 0.90 fatigue for domestic match within 48h
  Congestion modifier: 3 games in 7 days = × 0.88 for all players
```

## NBA connection intelligence

```
THE NBA-EUROLEAGUE PIPELINE AND TOKEN SIGNALS:

NBA DRAFT PROSPECT IN EUROLEAGUE:
  Player identified as top-5 NBA draft pick currently playing in EuroLeague
  Token signal: × 1.15 for their club (quality validation)
  Draft confirmation: × 1.10 (club produced top talent)
  Draft departure: × 0.90 (losing the commercial driver)

  ATM depends on: career peak, current form, market relevance
  Peak NBA star in EuroLeague: ATM 0.65-0.75 (reduced from NBA peak but higher than average)
  Recent NBA roster player: ATM 0.55-0.65
  
  Commercial value of NBA return signing:

    LTUI signal: + 5-8 for clubs signing notable NBA alumni

  EuroLeague MVP announcement (end of season):
    Winning club's token: + commercial narrative spike

    If MVP is NBA-linked (came from or going to NBA): amplified signal
```
