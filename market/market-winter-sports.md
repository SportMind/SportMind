# Winter Sports — SportMind Market Intelligence

## At a Glance
**Tier 3/4 — Niche with Geographic Concentration.** Winter sports (alpine skiing, ski
jumping, biathlon, cross-country) have passionate, concentrated audiences in Alpine
Europe, Scandinavia, and North America. The Olympics and World Championships are peak
moments. No fan tokens. Individual athlete tokens (Mikaela Shiffrin, Marco Odermatt)
are the most plausible structure given the individual sport nature.

---

## Fan Token Readiness Tier

**Tier 3/4 — Niche with Geographic Concentration**

Winter sports have genuine commercial depth in their core markets (Austria, Switzerland,
Norway, Germany) but very limited global reach. The Olympic cycle creates the same
structural challenge as athletics and swimming — 4-year commercial peaks with long
quiet periods. The Crystal Globe (season-long achievement award) creates a sustained
annual narrative, but viewership and commercial activity are seasonal by definition.

---

## 1. Global Revenue and Broadcasts

**FIS (International Ski Federation):** ~$80M/yr
**Alpine skiing broadcast:** Eurosport primary; ORF (Austria) dominant national broadcaster;
NRK (Norway); SRF (Switzerland)
**Biathlon World Cup:** One of the most-watched winter sports after alpine skiing;
strong German and Scandinavian followings

---

## 2. Fanbase and Demographics

**Global fan estimate:** ~300M (seasonally engaged)
**Core markets:** Austria (~3M), Switzerland (~2M), Norway (~4M — per-capita highest),
Germany (~8M), France (~5M)
**Age profile:** Median age ~38; affluent demographic (winter sports participation is expensive)
**Gender split:** ~55% male / ~45% female

---

## 3. Token Catalyst Events

**Olympic Winter Games:** 2026 (Milan-Cortina, Italy) is the next winter Olympics;
Italian market activation opportunity.
**Mikaela Shiffrin world record continuation:** The most decorated alpine skier in history;
ongoing record-breaking generates consistent media coverage.
**Marco Odermatt era:** Swiss skier establishing dominance — creates Swiss market engagement.
