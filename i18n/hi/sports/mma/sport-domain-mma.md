## लड़ाई की सप्ताह (Fight Week) — सबसे ज़रूरी signal

```
FIGHT WEEK TIMELINE:
  सोमवार–बुधवार: Training camp signals → form indicators
  बुधवार: Official weigh-in → BINARY CRITICAL SIGNAL
  गुरुवार: Face-off → psychological indicators  
  शुक्रवार: Weigh-in → final confirmation
  शनिवार: Fight night
```

### पेसाज (Weigh-in Signal)

| Weigh-in result | Token signal impact |
|---|---|
| आराम से weight बनाता है | Positive confirmation; signal hold |
| Weight बनाता है (≤0.5 lb difference) | Neutral |
| Weight miss → 1 घंटे का time | modifier: ×0.75 — significant negative |
| Final miss | modifier: ×0.72 — strong negative |
| Fighter swap (< 3 weeks notice) | Floor modifier: ×0.75 |
